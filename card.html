Arctic Fox

In winter they are white to blend in with the snow, while in the summer they change to brown!

  • Diet: Omnivores
  • Scientific Name: Vulpes lagopus
  • Size: The average head-and-body length of the male is 55 cm (22 in), with a range of 46 to 68 cm (18 to 27 in), while the female averages 52 cm (20 in) with a range of 41 to 55 cm (16 to 22 in).
  • Habitat: Tundra

Small body with thick fur everything about an Arctic fox helps it survive its harsh habitat. Even the bottom of its paws have hair to give the fox good traction. But for an Arctic fox the tail is extra useful, since the fox curls it around the face like a scarf.
